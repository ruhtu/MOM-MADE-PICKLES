#ABOUT US

Our Story

TANGY PICKLES was born out of a passion for creating delicious and high-quality pickles. Our founders, with a combined experience of over 20 years in the food industry, embarked on a journey to bring the finest pickles to customers who appreciate authentic taste and quality ingredients.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and ensure that every jar of TANGY PICKLES meets the highest standards of quality and flavor. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the pickle industry, and we continue to innovate and create new flavors to delight our customers.

Our Process

At TANGY PICKLES, we believe in using traditional pickling methods while incorporating modern techniques to ensure consistency and quality. Our process involves:

  • Sourcing fresh, locally grown ingredients
  • Hand-sorting and cleaning the produce
  • Creating small batches to ensure consistent flavor and texture
  • Using natural preservatives and no artificial colors or flavors
  • Sterilizing and sealing each jar for a safe and shelf-stable product
